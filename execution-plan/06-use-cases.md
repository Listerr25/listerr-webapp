## §7 Customer Lists — `/use-cases/customer-lists`

**Signature format — origin of the brand name.** No competitor has this natively.

H1: **Customer Lists — Listerr's Signature, First-of-Its-Kind Format**
Sub: Curated, ranked, interactive collections users browse, vote, and save. Perfect for discovery and editorial.

### List Formats (4 cards)
| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| Ranked Product Lists | "Top 10 Bestsellers This Week" — auto-updates from inventory |
| Curated Editorial Lists | "Best Skincare Routine for Monsoon" — editor-controlled |
| User-Voted Lists | "Community's Top Pick" badge drives social proof |
| Deal & Offer Lists | "Today's Flash Deals" — time-sensitive auto-refresh |

### Why Lists Are Powerful
- Format familiarity (BuzzFeed, ProductHunt, Amazon Movers)
- SEO value (ranks for "best [category]" queries)
- Low drop-off (users scroll longer than banners)
- Reusable (one list = homepage + PDP + emails + push)

## §9 AI Personalisation — `/use-cases/ai-personalisation`

H1: **Every User Sees the Content Most Likely to Convert Them**
Sub: Real-time AI scoring across Stories, Reels, and Lists. No manual segmentation rules.

### How It Works (4-step diagram)
1. User opens app → context captured (device, location, time, history)
2. AI scores all available content in real time
3. Highest-score content surfaces at top of feed
4. Every interaction retrains the model continuously

### Signal Categories (4 cards)
| Card | Examples |
|---|---|
| Behavioural | Pages visited, products viewed, searches, cart, orders, content engaged |
| Contextual | Time, day, device, location, weather |
| Demographic | Age range, gender, language, platform source |
| Campaign | Active promotions, inventory, margin goals — business rules |

### AI Tools Inside (bullet list)
- A/B Testing Engine (statistical significance)
- Multi-Armed Bandit (real-time traffic shifting)
- Dynamic Content Injection (name, city, last viewed product)
- Cohort Builder (lookalike segments)
