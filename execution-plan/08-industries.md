# 08 — Industry Pages

6 vertical pages under `/industries/`. Same template, different content per vertical.

## Shared industry template

1. **Hero** — H1 industry-specific tagline, sub-headline naming concrete buyer pain
2. **Top use cases for this industry** — 3 cards (subset of the 9 use cases)
3. **Industry-specific outcome metrics** — 3 stats
4. **Featured case study from this vertical**
5. **Recommended pricing tier** — single card highlight + link
6. **Relevant integrations** — logo cloud filtered to this vertical
7. **Bottom CTA** — vertical-tuned (e.g. "Talk to E-Commerce Team")
