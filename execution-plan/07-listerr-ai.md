Sub: Inbound and outbound calling powered by AI that knows your catalogue, your policies, and your customers.

### How It Works (7-step flow, blueprint §14)
1. Customer sees "Call to Order" in a Story / Reel / WhatsApp message
2. Call connects to AI Voice Agent — no hold, no IVR
3. AI greets by name (if known), understands natural language
4. AI accesses live catalogue, checks availability, quotes price, handles offers
